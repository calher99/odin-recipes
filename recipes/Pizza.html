Homemade Pizza

This is a survival pizza recipe that requires the most common ingredients you can find in a kitchen.

Dish Characteristics

The dish takes 30 minutes to be ready and you need an oven.

Ingredients

  • 400 gr flour
  • 200 ml of water
  • 2 teaspoons of extra virgin olive oil
  • Pizza ingredientes to choose

Cooking Steps

  1. Place frying pan at a temperature of 6 out of 10
  2. Pour a teaspoon of oil in a frying pan
  3. Chop onion and chorizo into nail size pieces
  4. Leave onion and chorizo in the pan until golden color
  5. Boil the pasta for 10 minutes
  6. Once chorizo and onions are golden pour tomatoe sauce
  7. Leave tomato mix in fire until a bit less liquid
  8. Place pasta with tomato mix for 5 minutes and it will be ready
